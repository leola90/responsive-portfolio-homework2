Leo La is currently working at the Greater Houston Partnership as an IT technician. With this course, he will build a portfolio of web applications, learning about different programming languages such as HTML5, CSS3, Java, JavaScript, MySQL, and much more. During his free time, Leo likes to design and play with machines on his Raspberry Pi. He also enjoys solving logic puzzles and traveling around the world.
